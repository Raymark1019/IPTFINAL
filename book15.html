Angels & Demons

Summary

Before the events of The Da Vinci Code, Robert Langdon is called to investigate the murder of a

scientist at CERN, the European Organization for Nuclear Research. The murder is linked to the

Illuminati, a secret society that has been thought extinct for centuries. Langdon, along with

scientist Vittoria Vetra, uncovers a plot to destroy the Vatican using antimatter, a powerful

substance stolen from CERN. As the clock ticks down to an explosive conclusion, Langdon and

Vetra race to stop the Illuminati's plan, navigating Vatican history, religious symbols, and

dangerous secret conspiracies. The novel explores themes of science vs. religion and the search

for truth.
